3d-printing

A lot of things can be 3d printed today. From toys to industrial tools, or even houses. 3d printing has become very popular to all sorts of hobbyists.It is fun, cool, and can be used in a lot of fields. To understand to awesomeness of the 3d printer, you must know how it works. Basically the 3d printer is made in a cartesian style. This means that there is an x-axis, y-axis, and z-axis
